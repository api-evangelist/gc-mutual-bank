# G&C Mutual Bank

> G&C Mutual Bank is an Australian customer-owned mutual bank and authorised deposit-taking institution (ADI). It merged with Unity Bank (legal merge 7 March 2025; combined entity renamed Unity Bank Limited from 1 July 2025), and gcmutual.bank now redirects to unity.bank, while the G&C-branded digital-banking host (gcmutualbank.com.au) that serves its Open Banking API remains operational during brand consolidation. Under Australia's Consumer Data Right (CDR / Open Banking) the bank exposes a public, unauthenticated Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the DSB Consumer Data Standards.

## APIs
- [CDR Product Reference Data API](https://unity.bank/about-us/corporate-information/open-banking/): Public, unauthenticated GET /banking/products and /banking/products/{productId} at https://ibank.gcmutualbank.com.au/openbanking/cds-au/v1 — confirmed HTTP 200 with 25 products (74 total) at x-v 4/5.
